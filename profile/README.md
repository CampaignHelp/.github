# CampaignHelp

**Production, technology, and helpdesk support for progressive advocacy organizations.**

We're a small consulting team that helps progressive nonprofits run the systems behind their digital programs — ActionKit, Action Network, and the long tail of tools that hold campaigns together.

When a fundraising email needs to go out tonight and the merge tags are broken, when a redirect from your old donate page is sending traffic into a void, when your action page works in QA but not in production — that's us.

## Who we work with

Progressive 501(c)(3)s and (c)(4)s, labor unions, electoral committees, and advocacy coalitions. Teams that need experienced tech and operations help without a full-time hire.

## What we publish here

Tools we built for client work and open-sourced because the rest of the movement could use them too:

- **[ak-health-check](https://github.com/CampaignHelp/ak-health-check)** — A dashboard of SQL checks for ActionKit instances. Catches the silent stuff before it shows up in a board report.
- **[ak-redirect-snippets](https://github.com/CampaignHelp/ak-redirect-snippets)** — Tested Django-template snippets for the redirects, splash pages, and one-off conditionals that every AK org ends up writing.

More to come. If your org runs on ActionKit or Action Network and you've got a problem you can't find a vendor for, that's the kind of thing we work on.
